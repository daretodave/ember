ember · brand

v1 · canonical mosaic reference

the mosaic is the brand mark. wherever ember needs a mark — header chrome, favicon, og card, social — the same 6×10 grid does the job. there is no separate logo. there is no wordmark-only treatment. the brand is the practice rendered.

01

the brand mark

a 60-day practice rendered: 6 columns × 10 rows of 16px tiles with a 4px gap. shown here at hero scale (32px tile) so the geometry reads clearly. the mark is identical in both modes — only the paper changes around it.

02

three sizes · three uses

tile geometry is fixed (square, no radius). only the tile and gap scale. the proportions of tile-to-gap (4:1) are preserved at every size so the mark looks like itself.

24px tile tile 24 · gap 6 · grid 174×294

32px tile tile 32 · gap 8 · grid 224×392

03

tile states · the only four

the mosaic encodes exactly four states. there is no "in-progress," no "draft," no "saved-but-not-published" — the model is binary at the tile level (entry exists or not) with a single qualifier (this entry is public). today is a temporary state, swapped to "filled" or "published" at the user's local midnight.

empty
no entry for this day.
fill none · border 1px ink-faint
radius 0
filled
a private entry exists.
fill accent · border accent
radius 0
today
filled, plus a 2px ring of paper.
outline 2px paper, offset -1px
a quiet "you are here"
published
filled, plus a 4px inset of accent-2.
opt-in, per entry, always reversible.
never implies a mass-publish toggle

the mosaic shows what is, not what isn't. empty tiles are quiet, not accusatory. a long streak of empty tiles is information, not failure.

06

lockups · two, no more

the mosaic alone is the canonical brand mark. for header chrome the small lockup pairs a tiny mosaic glyph with the lowercase wordmark. there is no third lockup. the wordmark never appears alone.

ember
header lockup small mosaic glyph (8px tile · 2px gap)
paired with the 24px serif wordmark
used in the global header on every page
mark alone the full mosaic, no wordmark
used on /log, og card, favicon source
this is the canonical brand mark
07

don't

five places the mark would drift if left unattended. these are non-negotiable.

×
ember
don't use the wordmark alone.
there is no wordmark-only treatment. the mosaic always sits with it (or alone).
×
don't add an illustration.
the mosaic is the only graphic in the entire product. no flames, no candles, no abstract marks.
×
don't add shadow or glow.
the mark is flat. depth comes from the page, not the mark. no drop-shadow, no inner-glow, no halo.
×
don't gradient the field.
no gradient backgrounds anywhere in the system. the mark sits on plain paper or plain dark paper. that is the rule.
×
don't round the tiles.
tile radius is 0. always. circles read as social signal (likes, dots, stickers); squares read as a calendar.
×
12-day streak
+1 today · keep it going
don't count.
no streak count. no "+N today." no "you've practiced N days." the mosaic shows what is.
